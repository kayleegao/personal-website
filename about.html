About me.

Hi, I'm Kaylee.

CCIT & CS & MATH Graduate @ UTM | MDEI Recent Graduate @ UWaterloo

👋🏻 Hi there! I'm Yifei Gao (Kaylee), a multi-disciplinary UX designer based in Shanghai, China. I am an alumni at University of Toronto Mississauga, majored in Communication, Culture, Information & Technology and Computer Science and minored in Mathematical Sciences. My passion for UX design had led me to complete a master's degree in Digital Experience Innovation program at the University of Waterloo.

👩‍💻 I have interned in three distinct fields: marketing, backend development, and interaction design. These experiences correspond to the different majors I pursued in undergraduate and graduate studies. It is because of the different internship experiences and the fusion of computer science and design courses, that I was able to find my passion for UX and interaction design. With a multi-disciplinary background, I combine my strength in programming and design to create user-centered products.

🌟 As a designer with frontend development skills, I independently designed and developed this portfolio website. I value accessability, the site is responsive and incorporates a smooth user experience across all types of devices.
