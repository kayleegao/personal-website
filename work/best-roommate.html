UX/UI design

In China, rising housing prices and a huge population are putting constant pressure on young people. Many of them choose to rent a house instead of buying one. In this case, the needs of finding a roommate and an apartment is huge in the society. Since roommates are people who need to get along day and night, people often think carefully about the choice of roommates. However, lack of understanding of others in a short period of time may lead them to find roommates who are not suitable for each other. Best roommate is an app that can match roommates and suitable apartments according to preferences, living habits and various requirements. It can help people efficiently find their satisfactory life partners and residences in the fast-paced era of China.

China's rental market.

In recent years, with the rise in housing prices, more and more people choose to rent apartments, and inevitable process is to find roommates. In addition, as people get married at a later age, they have also been in demand for longer periods of time of renting. The increase in urban populations has also made finding roommates more difficult. According to this trend, the proportion of people looking for roommates will gradually increase in the future.

Most renters are young people under the age of 30, so they are more receptive to software to find roommates. In this case, Best Roommate - an app that helps young people find ideal apartments and roommates in the same city will be in increasing market demand.
