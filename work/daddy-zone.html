Men usually have the stereotype that they do not take care of others as well as women do. They are sometimes looked down upon in some culture when they need to take care of parenting alone due to various external factors, while the diverse culture will continue to expand the problem. Isolation and helplessness will further increase their financial and psychological burden. Here I bring a solution for the future trend - Daddy Zone, a platform where dads can share their stories and parenting tips on the internet to gain a sense of belonging and form a stronger group.

Dads in the society.

With the development of society, more and more men have assumed the responsibility of caring for their children, especially in gay and single dad families. Under this phenomenon, Dadflueners continue to spread along with social media. Dad-related content is some of the most viral on the internet. Hashtags such as #Dadlife and #Fatherhood appear by the millions on social media. However, it is still a minority in most countries. Plus, there is a debate on whether online displays of fatherly virtue represent or translate into better parenting.

Men usually have the stereotype that they do not take care of others as well as women do. When it comes to an inevitable situation, what males have to take the responsibility of parenting, as a minority, they could barely gain experience from those who are facing the same situation. As society becomes more and more open, people are becoming more tolerant of sexual orientation, and the divorce rate has also increased. It would lead to an increase in the phenomenon of males needing to care about parenting.

Dads need a place to share & communicate.

To change people's perceptions and increase inclusiveness to fathers, I believe a community for fathers to gather and share experiences of parenting is helpful. Daddy Zone App is a social platform that constructs various communities to let users share their lives and baby care tips. Users can join different zones based on their situations. They are allowed to share their experience, thoughts, and baby care tips that relate to the zone topic. If they find a post informative, they can like, comment, or collect the post. The "collect" feature functions as favorites. Once a post has been collected, the user can find the post on their profile page. In addition, users can use the message feature if they want to communicate with someone. Although every single father is ordinary, they are able to gather together and form a stronger community in Daddy Zone.

Real-world scenario & persona.

The following is a real-world scenario. Mike is a single dad living in a small town. Due to the underdevelopment of medical technology, his wife died of dystocia, and he only has a baby to accompany him. Since then, Mike has taken the responsibility of taking care of and raising the baby. However, he is isolated because being a single dad is rare in the town. Due to the inflation, his current salary was not enough to support their living. Over time, under the double pressure of the economy and family, Mike feels that he needs to find a community with people in a similar situation. He downloads Daddy Zone App and joins a community called "single dad," and connects with many other fathers who are facing the same issue. After they share their stories and tips, Mike is benefited a lot and becomes more optimistic.

Design process & methodology.

01
Reddit

I referred to Reddit as the concept of the app. Since fathers in a dilemma need a sense of belongings, a social app that emphasizes communities like Reddit would be the most suitable way of presenting.

02
Ten Heuristics for Interface Design

Visibility of system status: the buttons with two states are, such as "follow" and "following" or "join" and "joined". They inform the user the current state of whether they have followed a user or joined a community.
When posting a new thread, there is feedback indicating whether it has been successfully posted or saved to draft.
To emphasize consistency, the app uses blue as the primary color and Avenir as the font.
